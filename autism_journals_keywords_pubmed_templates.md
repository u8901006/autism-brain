# Autism Journals / Scope / Q1–Q2 / Common Keywords / PubMed Search Templates

_Last updated: 2026-04-13_

## Notes
- This list is designed for **autism-related work across medicine, psychology, and neuroscience**.
- **Q1/Q2** status can vary by database and subject category. In this file, quartile labels are simplified to the **latest broadly available public ranking snapshots** checked on 2026-04-13.
- I prioritized journals that are either:
  1. **core autism journals**, or  
  2. **high-yield adjacent journals** where autism papers commonly appear.

## 1) Core autism journals

| Journal | Topic positioning | Q1/Q2 | Common autism keywords |
|---|---|---:|---|
| **Molecular Autism** | Molecular, genetic, neurobiological, biomarker, translational, and clinical autism research | **Q1** | autism spectrum disorder, ASD, genetics, transcriptomics, biomarkers, neuroinflammation, synaptic function, animal model, neurodevelopment |
| **Autism Research** | Broad interdisciplinary autism journal spanning basic science, clinical studies, cognition, intervention, epidemiology, and services | **Q1** | autism spectrum disorder, ASD, social communication, restricted repetitive behaviors, early detection, biomarkers, intervention, co-occurring conditions |
| **Journal of Autism and Developmental Disorders** | Clinical, developmental, diagnostic, educational, and treatment-oriented autism research across the lifespan | **Q1** | autism diagnosis, developmental disorders, phenotype, assessment, intervention, adaptive functioning, sensory processing, family outcomes |
| **Autism** | Practical and policy-relevant autism research with strong applied, social, developmental, and lived-experience relevance | **Q1** | autistic individuals, quality of life, education, participation, mental health, services, inclusion, social communication |
| **Review Journal of Autism and Developmental Disorders** | Critical reviews and evidence syntheses across autism science, diagnosis, treatment, education, and neuroscience | **Q1** | systematic review, scoping review, meta-analysis, autism spectrum disorder, intervention, prevalence, etiology, cognition |
| **Research in Autism Spectrum Disorders** | Empirical and review work across genetic, neurobiological, cognitive, behavioral, and intervention levels | **Q2** | autism spectrum disorders, cognition, behavior, intervention, developmental trajectory, communication, repetitive behavior |
| **Focus on Autism and Other Developmental Disabilities** | Practice-facing educational and treatment content for clinicians, teachers, service providers, and families | **Q1–Q2** | school-based intervention, transition, classroom support, parent training, special education, functional communication, developmental disabilities |

## 4) PubMed search templates

> Replace bracketed items such as `[age group]`, `[topic]`, or `[method]` with your own terms.

### Template 1 — Broad autism search
```text
("autism spectrum disorder"[Title/Abstract] OR autism[Title/Abstract] OR ASD[Title/Abstract])
```

### Template 2 — Autism + age group
```text
(("autism spectrum disorder"[Title/Abstract] OR autism[Title/Abstract] OR ASD[Title/Abstract]))
AND
([age group][Title/Abstract] OR child*[Title/Abstract] OR adolescen*[Title/Abstract] OR adult*[Title/Abstract])
```

### Template 3 — Autism + symptom / phenotype
```text
(("autism spectrum disorder"[Title/Abstract] OR autism[Title/Abstract] OR ASD[Title/Abstract]))
AND
("[topic]"[Title/Abstract] OR "social communication"[Title/Abstract] OR "sensory processing"[Title/Abstract] OR "restricted repetitive behaviors"[Title/Abstract])
```

### Template 4 — Autism + comorbidity
```text
(("autism spectrum disorder"[Title/Abstract] OR autism[Title/Abstract] OR ASD[Title/Abstract]))
AND
("[comorbidity]"[Title/Abstract] OR anxiety[Title/Abstract] OR depression[Title/Abstract] OR ADHD[Title/Abstract] OR sleep[Title/Abstract])
```

### Template 5 — Autism + intervention
```text
(("autism spectrum disorder"[Title/Abstract] OR autism[Title/Abstract] OR ASD[Title/Abstract]))
AND
("[intervention]"[Title/Abstract] OR intervention*[Title/Abstract] OR treatment[Title/Abstract] OR therapy[Title/Abstract] OR "parent-mediated"[Title/Abstract])
```

### Template 6 — Autism + neuroscience / biomarker methods
```text
(("autism spectrum disorder"[Title/Abstract] OR autism[Title/Abstract] OR ASD[Title/Abstract]))
AND
("[method]"[Title/Abstract] OR EEG[Title/Abstract] OR ERP[Title/Abstract] OR fMRI[Title/Abstract] OR "eye tracking"[Title/Abstract] OR biomarker*[Title/Abstract])
```

### Template 7 — Autism + genetics / molecular biology
```text
(("autism spectrum disorder"[Title/Abstract] OR autism[Title/Abstract] OR ASD[Title/Abstract]))
AND
(genetic*[Title/Abstract] OR genomic*[Title/Abstract] OR transcriptomic*[Title/Abstract] OR epigenetic*[Title/Abstract] OR "molecular"[Title/Abstract])
```

### Template 8 — Autism + systematic review / meta-analysis
```text
(("autism spectrum disorder"[Title/Abstract] OR autism[Title/Abstract] OR ASD[Title/Abstract]))
AND
("systematic review"[Title/Abstract] OR "meta-analysis"[Publication Type] OR meta-analysis[Title/Abstract] OR review[Title/Abstract])
```

### Template 9 — Autism + journal targeting filter
```text
(("autism spectrum disorder"[Title/Abstract] OR autism[Title/Abstract] OR ASD[Title/Abstract]))
AND
("[topic]"[Title/Abstract])
AND
("Journal of Autism and Developmental Disorders"[Journal] OR "Autism Research"[Journal] OR "Molecular Autism"[Journal] OR "Autism"[Journal])
```

### Template 10 — High-specificity clinical search
```text
(("autism spectrum disorder"[Title/Abstract] OR autism[Title/Abstract] OR ASD[Title/Abstract]))
AND
(diagnos*[Title/Abstract] OR assess*[Title/Abstract] OR treatment[Title/Abstract] OR intervention*[Title/Abstract])
AND
(clinic*[Title/Abstract] OR patient*[Title/Abstract] OR outpatient[Title/Abstract] OR hospital[Title/Abstract])
```

## 5) Quick use suggestions

### If you are searching for:
- **core clinical autism papers** → start with  
  **Journal of Autism and Developmental Disorders**, **Autism Research**, **Autism**
- **molecular / biomarker / genetics papers** → start with  
  **Molecular Autism**, **Biological Psychiatry**, **Journal of Neurodevelopmental Disorders**
- **developmental psychology / child psychiatry / longitudinal work** → start with  
  **JCPP**, **Autism Research**, **Journal of Autism and Developmental Disorders**
- **reviews to quickly map the field** → start with  
  **Review Journal of Autism and Developmental Disorders**
- **practice-facing school / family / service papers** → start with  
  **Research in Autism Spectrum Disorders**, **Focus on Autism and Other Developmental Disabilities**
